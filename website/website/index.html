The Challenge

Kenya's farmers plant
without data

Over 75% of Kenya's food is produced by smallholder farmers making planting decisions based on tradition — not evidence. The result is preventable losses worth billions of shillings every year.

🌧️
Unpredictable rainfall
Climate change has made seasonal rainfall increasingly erratic, causing yield failures that data-driven planning could prevent.
📊
No prediction tools
Existing advisory platforms give generic advice. None provide personalised, county-level yield forecasts before the season begins.
🌱
Misallocated inputs
Without forecasts, farmers over- or under-apply fertiliser and seeds — wasting money and reducing yields simultaneously.
🏛️
Reactive government response
County governments react to food crises after they happen. Yield predictions enable proactive resource deployment.
The Solution

How AgroInsight works

Four steps from raw data to actionable yield predictions.

01
Data Collection
Historical yield, weather, and soil data collected from KNBS, Kenya Met, and FAO covering 10 years across Kenyan counties.
KNBS · FAO · NASA
02
Data Processing
Raw data is cleaned, standardised, and enriched with engineered features like rainfall deviation and soil fertility indices.
Pandas · NumPy
03
ML Training
Three models trained and compared — Linear Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost. The best model is selected automatically.
XGBoost · Scikit-learn
04
Prediction Dashboard
Select your county, crop, and season. Get an instant yield forecast with a confidence rating and farming recommendations.
Streamlit · Live
What You Get

Everything a farmer
needs to decide

🔮
Yield Prediction
Enter your county, crop, season, and weather parameters. Get a predicted yield in kg/ha with a performance rating — Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor.
💡
Smart Recommendations
Every prediction comes with personalised farming advice — irrigation guidance, soil pH correction tips, and fertiliser recommendations.
📈
Historical Trends
Visualise how yields have changed over 10 years across counties and crops. Identify which regions perform best for each crop type.

Data Explorer
Browse and filter the full training dataset. View distribution charts, compare counties, and download data for your own analysis.
⚡
Instant Results
No waiting. Adjust sliders for rainfall, temperature, soil pH, and area planted — predictions update in real time as you explore scenarios.
🆓
Completely Free
